Hi! My Name is Nicole Kurtz.

I am originally from Chapel Hill, North Carolina where I obtained my Graphic Design associates in 2016. I worked as a graphic designer for four years before pursuing my passion in Computer Science at Portland State University (PSU) in 2020. I worked as a Lab Facilitator for the lower division computer science courses at PSU, leading presentations on lab topics to support students with programming the concepts they were learning in the classroom. The focus of the labs was on data structures and abstract data types in C++.

In 2022, I took 6-months off of school to work as a full-time Software Development intern at Cambia Health Solutions for a 6 month period. During this time, I led the design and creation of an AWS DynamoDB table and C# client for use of tracing the lifecycle of events through applications. Additionally, this project required record creation throughout the teams applications at event state changes. Following completion of the client, I developed API endpoints and a user interface for querying DynamoDB. Upon completion of the full-time internship, Cambia offered me to continue working part-time when I returned to school until the completion of my degree.

Through my former experience as a Graphic Designer as well as my work experience at Cambia, I have formed a passion for Full Stack Development. Additionally, I have a passion for creating software that manages and analyzes large amounts of data.
